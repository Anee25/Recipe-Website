Kala Chana Chat

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black chickpeas (kala chana)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 cucumber (optional)
  • 1–2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • ½ tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon juice (1–2 tsp)
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • (Optional) boiled potato pieces

Method

  1. Soak kala chana overnight and boil until soft.
  2. Drain excess water.
  3. Add chopped onion, tomato, cucumber, and green chilies.
  4. Mix in chaat masala, cumin powder, and salt.
  5. Add lemon juice and mix well.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander.
  7. Serve fresh and spicy! 😋

Serving Tips

  • Eat as a healthy snack
  • Great for weight loss diet
  • Can be served as street-style chaat
