Almond Milk

Ingredients

  • Makes 1 cup

Milk

  • 1/4 cup almonds,soaked
  • 1 cup Water

Preparation

  • Soak 1/4 cup almonds for 6 hours.

Method

  1. Peel the almonds (this is optional, it simply makes the milk creamier).
  2. Place the peeled almonds into a blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Add 1 cup of fresh water to the blender and blend again, until smooth.
  4. Almond milk can be used right away, or stored in the fridge for 1 to 2 days.
