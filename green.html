Green Chutney

Ingredients

  • Makes 3/4 cup

Chutney

  • 1 cup coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves
  • 1 small green chili
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or 1⁄2 cup raw mango, roughly chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon rock salt
  • 3 tablespoons water

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Use immediately or store in the fridge for 2 to 3 days.
