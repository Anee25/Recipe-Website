Chocolate Brownie

🥄Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter (melted)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs (or ½ cup curd for eggless)
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • ⅓ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • Chocolate chips or nuts (optional)
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix melted butter and sugar well.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla essence, mix until smooth.
  4. In another bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Combine dry and wet ingredients.
  6. Add chocolate chips if you like.
  7. Pour into a greased tray.
  8. Bake for 20–25 minutes.
  9. Let it cool, then cut into squares.
🍫 Tips
  • Don’t overbake for fudgy brownies
  • Add walnuts for crunch
  • Serve with ice cream for best taste 🍦
