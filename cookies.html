Stuffed Cookies

Ingredients

  • Makes 8

Chocolate Stuffing

  • 1/4 cup almonds, soaked
  • 2 tablespoons cacao powder
  • 1⁄2 cup seedless dates, tightly packed
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • pinch of rock salt

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour or oats flour
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon rock salt

Wet Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup almond butter (recipe below)
  • 3/4 cup jaggery powder

Almond Milk

  • 3 tablespoons almonds, soaked and peeled
  • 1/3 cup water

Topping

  • 2 tablespoons chopped nuts

Preparation

  • Soak 1/4 cup almonds for 6 hours (for stuffing).
  • Soak 3 tbsp almonds (for almond milk).
  • Prepare 1/4 cup almond butter (recipe below).

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients of chocolate stuffing into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Place all the dry ingredients into a mixing bowl and mix well.
  3. Place almond butter and jaggery powder into another mixing bowl. Mix well to combine everything.
  4. For the almond milk, place the soaked and peeled almonds and water into a blender and blend until smooth.
  5. Add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix. Add the almond milk in parts and knead the dough. Cover and keep it in the refrigerator for 15 minutes.
  6. Preheat the oven at 180°C for 10 minutes and divide the dough into 8 balls. Stuff each ball with chocolate stuffing and reshape into a ball. Place on parchment paper in a baking tray.
  7. With the back of a spoon, gently press the balls. Top the cookies with chopped nuts. Make sure to leave enough space in between each cookie dough. Bake the cookies at 180°C for 10 minutes.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool for about 10 minutes.
  9. Serve and enjoy! You can store them in an airtight container.
