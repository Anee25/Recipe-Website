Masala Makhana

Ingredients

  • Makes 2 cups

Makhana

  • 2 cups for nuts(makhana)
  • 1 teaspoon jaggery powder
  • ½ teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • ½ teaspoon black salt
  • ½ teaspoon asafetida
  • ¼ teaspoon rock salt
Method
  1. Heat a pot and add fox nuts. Roast for 10minutes on low to medium flame until crisp.
  2. Add jaggery powder and roasted cumin powder to the same pot and mix.
  3. Switch off the stove. Add black salt, asafoetida, and rock salt. Mix everything together.
  4. Take out these masala makhana on a plateto cool down.
  5. Serve and enjoy! Store them in an airtightcontainer for 2 weeks.
