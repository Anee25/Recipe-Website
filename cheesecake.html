Mango Cheesecake

Crust

  • 3–4 cup almonds, roasted
  • ½ cup seedledd dates
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Pinch of Rock salt

Filling

  • 1 cup cashews, soaked
  • 1 cup mango puree
  • 8 dates, seedless
  • 2 tablespoons jaggery powder
  • (1-4) teaspoon lemon juice

Toppings

  • ½ cup mango puree
  • Mango slices of 1 mango
  • 1 tablespoon pomegranate
  • Few mint sprigs

Preparation

  • Soak 1 cup of cashews in water for 6 hours.
Method
  1. For the crust, place all the ingredients into a blender and blend until crumbly. If you overblend it, it will become sticky and not yield a good texture.
  2. Grease an 8 inch cake tin with cold pressed coconut oil. Spread the crust evenly and press down lightly with your hands. Keep it in the freezer for 10 minutes.
  3. For the filling, place all the ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth. The more you blend, the creamier it will become.
  4. Pour the filling on top of the crust and tap the tin to even out the surface of the filling. Freeze for 6 hours until set.
  5. Before serving, let the cake thaw for 10 minutes so it's soft to slice. Add the toppings, slice, and serve!
