Coconut Butter

Ingredients

  • Makes ½ cup

Butter

  • 1 dry coconut(gola)

Method

  1. Scrape off the external brown layer of a dry coconut (this is optional, it simply makes the butter white).
  2. Chop the dry coconut into small pieces.
  3. Transfer the pieces into a blender and blend for 5 to 7 minutes. Blend for only 15 seconds at a time to avoid overheating.
  4. You may feel like it will never turn into butter, but soon the powder will turn into a buttery substance.
  5. Transfer the butter into a jar. The butter can be stored for 5 to 7 days.
