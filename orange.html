Orange Juice

🥄Ingredients

  • 3–4 fresh oranges
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

🥤Method

  1. Peel the oranges and remove seeds.
  2. Blend or squeeze using a juicer.
  3. Strain if needed.
  4. Add honey and mix well.
  5. Serve chilled.

💪Benefits

  • Rich in Vitamin C
  • Boosts immunity
  • Good for skin health
