Chickpea Curry (Chana Masala)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled chickpeas
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Method

  1. Heat oil and add cumin seeds.
  2. Sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes.
  4. Add spices and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add chickpeas and simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

A chickpea curry is a popular, protein-rich legume dish served with rice or roti.
