Aloo Matar Malai

Ingredients

  • Serves 2

Base

  • 2 green cardamom pods
  • 2 cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 cup chopped potato
  • 1/2 cup green peas (fresh)
  • 3/4 cup water

Coconut Purée

  • 1⁄2 cup grated coconut
  • 1/4 teaspoon green chili
  • 1 teaspoon rock salt
  • 1⁄2 cup water

Other Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons kasuri methi

Method

  1. Heat a pot and add the cardamom, cloves, and cumin. Sauté for 2 minutes on medium flame until the spices become fragrant.
  2. Add the potato, green peas, and water to the same pot. Cover, and cook for 15 minutes on medium flame until soft.
  3. Place all the coconut purée ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  4. Once the vegetables are cooked, add the kasuri methi to the pot. Cook for 2 minutes while stirring.
  5. Switch off the stove, add the coconut purée, and stir. Cover for 10 minutes, then serve warm with roti or rice.
