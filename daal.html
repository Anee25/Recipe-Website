Daal Tarkari (Dal Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (toor dal / moong dal)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

Method

  1. Wash dal and boil it with turmeric until soft.
  2. Heat oil/ghee in a pan.
  3. Add cumin seeds, garlic, and ginger.
  4. Add onions and cook until golden brown.
  5. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until soft.
  6. Add boiled dal and mix well.
  7. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serving Ideas

  • Best with rice (dal chawal)
  • Also good with roti or naan
  • Add pickle or papad for extra taste
