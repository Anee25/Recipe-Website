Kulcha

Ingredients

  • Makes 5

Kulcha

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 11⁄2 teaspoons jaggery powder
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon rock salt
  • 1⁄2 cup warm water

Topping

  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander
  • 1 teaspoon roasted jakhiya seeds or black sesame seeds

Method

  1. Combine all the kulcha ingredients, except the warm water, in a large mixing bowl. Stir well.
  2. Add the warm water, and knead the dough. Cover the bowl with a wet cloth, and let it rest for 20 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into 5 balls. Dust a rolling board with some flour, and flatten each ball.
  4. Sprinkle some coriander and jakhiya seeds on top, then roll each ball into a 4.5 inch circle with a rolling pin (belan).
  5. Heat a tawa, and cook each kulcha on a low to medium flame until golden spots appear.
  6. Serve warm with Sabzi.
