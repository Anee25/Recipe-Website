Chocolate Ice Cream

🥄Ingredients

  • Serves 12
Ice Cream
  • 3 cups cashew,soaked
  • 5 cups thick coconut milk
  • 1½ cup jaggery powder
  • (3-4) cup cacao powder
  • 1½ teaspoons vanilla powder
  • (1-4) teaspoon rock salt
Topping
  • ½ cup nuts,roasted and chopped
Preparation
  1. Soak 3 cups of cashew for 6 hours.
  2. Prepare thick coconut milk by blending 5 cups coconut with 4½ cups water & then sieving it.
Method
  1. Place all the ice cream ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth. Make sure it is blended properly.
  2. Pour the mixture into a glass or steel container, and let it freeze for about 6 hours in the freezer.
  3. Before serving, let the ice cream thaw for 10 minutes, until it becomes soft enough to scoop.
  4. Add the toppings and serve cold.
