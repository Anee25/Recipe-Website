Amla Juice (Indian Gooseberry Juice)

🥄Ingredients

  • 4–5 fresh amla (Indian gooseberries)
  • 1–2 cups water
  • 1–2 tsp honey or sugar (optional)
  • ½ tsp black salt (optional)
  • ½ tsp roasted cumin powder (optional)
  • Mint leaves (optional for freshness)

🥤Method

  1. Wash and cut amla into small pieces.
  2. Remove seeds.
  3. 3Blend amla with water until smooth.
  4. Strain the juice (optional if you want smooth juice).
  5. Add honey, black salt, and cumin powder.
  6. Mix well and serve chilled.

💪Health Benefits

  • Boosts immunity
  • Good for skin and hair
  • Helps digestion
  • Rich in Vitamin C
  • Detoxifies the body
