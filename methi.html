Methi Masala Roti

Ingredients

  • Makes 6

Dough

  • 2 cups finely chopped, fresh fenugreek leaves (methi)
  • 1/4 cup jaggery powder
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon rock salt
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients (except the wheat flour) in a large mixing bowl, using your hands.
  2. Add the wheat flour to the bowl and knead the mixture into a soft dough. Use a little water if required.
  3. Divide the dough into 5 to 6 balls and flatten them.
  4. Roll the balls into flat rotis using a rolling pin (belan).
  5. Heat a tawa and cook the rotis over a low to medium flame.
  6. Serve warm with peanut curd raita or sabzi.
