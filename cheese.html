Cashew Cheese

Ingredients

  • Makes ½ cup

Cheese

  • 1/2 cup chopped cauliflower, boiled
  • 1⁄2 cup cashews, soaked
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dry oregano
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon rock salt
  • 1 teaspoon water

Preparation

  • Soak ½ cup cashews in water for 6 hours.

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Use immediately or store in the fridge for 2 to 3 days.
