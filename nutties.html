Chocolate Nutties

Ingredients

  • Makes ½ cups

Nutties

  • ¼ cup jaggery powder
  • 2 tablespoon water
  • 3 tablespoons cacao powder
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds, roasted
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup almonds, roasted
  • ¼ cup cashews, roasted
  • pinch of rock salt
Method
  1. Heat a saucepan. Add jaggery, water and cook for 8 minutes on low flame until bubbles start appearing and the consistency becomes a little thick. Keep stirring in between.
  2. Add cacao powder, chia seeds, and vanilla. Mix and let it cook for a few more minutes until a thick caramel like consistency is formed.
  3. Add roasted nuts and mix well to evenly coat the nuts with chocolate.
  4. Switch off the stove, add rock salt and mix well. Separate the nuts by breaking the lumps.
  5. Take out these nutties on a plate to cool down.
  6. Store them in an airtight container for 2 weeks.
